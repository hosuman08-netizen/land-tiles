On the map itself: arrow keys pan, +/− zoom, and the zoom buttons are reachable by tab. On touch, one finger scrolls the page and two fingers move the map. On desktop, hold ⌘/Ctrl to zoom with the wheel.

Market

Appraisal (NLV) is what the valuation engine thinks a block is worth. The order book is what an agent will actually pay. The two disagree on purpose.

Vault

Everything your land produces while you are away — resources, lease income, jewels — and everything it is currently building.

Voice claim

Speak while you claim and the microphone's measured energy becomes a permanent property of that block: higher starting vitality and aura, and a larger bonus on every structure you ever build there.

Energy is measured from the live waveform (RMS), never guessed. Nothing is locked behind the microphone — the silent path gives the baseline 0.35.

The claim targets the block you last opened if it is never-minted, otherwise the nearest never-minted block to the centre of your map view.

Ledger

Every claim, build, lease, offer and settlement, in order.

E2Verse is a fictional simulation. Blocks, agents, prices and yields are generated by the page you are reading. No real property, currency, security or account is involved, and nothing here is financial advice. 18+.
